Stokipa Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 July 2026

Stokipa is a messaging assistant that helps shopkeepers track inventory and sales over WhatsApp. This policy explains, in plain language, what we store and how we handle it.

What we store

  • Your phone number — so we can recognise you and reply to your messages.
  • The shop information you send us — your inventory (products, quantities, prices) and the sales and restocks you report.

We only store what you choose to send. We use this information solely to run the service for you — recording your stock, calculating sales, and sending the alerts and reports you ask for.

We do not sell or share your data

We do not sell your information, and we do not share it with third parties for advertising or marketing. Your data is used only to operate Stokipa.

Deleting your data

You can ask us to delete your data at any time. Email us at privacy@stokipa.com from, or mentioning, the phone number linked to your account, and we will remove your stored inventory, sales, and phone number.
